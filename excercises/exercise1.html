About Me-Michael Oliveri

Introduction

I am currently a Junior at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, majoring in Informatics with a concentration in data science. Formerly a computer science major I am eager to experience ways in which I can attribute my learnings to the outside world. The ever-changing landscape of Information Sciences is an environment that I cannot wait to plunge into after my graduation.

Influences & Experiences

Growing up with the rapid development of technology, a father who works in the field, and a passion for all things computers has naturally led me down the path of studying Informatics. Having a rather technologically inept family (sorry guys) always led to me being the one having to troubleshoot their issues. And honestly, I am grateful, because without those experiences I wouldn't have been able to gain the problem-solving skills that I am so proud of today.

Though I am very grateful for my family, and the support they have given me for all of these years. I am particularly thankful for my older brother. When I was in high school, and admittedly a bit aimless, he was the one who encouraged me to pursue a degree in Informatics. Due to him giving me the confidence to pursue this degree, I am able to thrive in a field I love. For that, I will be forever thankful.

Hobbies & Interests

When I'm not sitting behind a computer and coding until my fingers fall off, I enjoy reading, traveling, and spending time with my friends. I find that with all the time I spend looking at a screen, activities that allow me to break away from my computer for a while are invaluable.

Though I am trying to break into new hobbies like art and video editing (despite admittedly arriving at the scene a bit late), I will always find comfort in a nice vacation, a captivating book, and quality time with my family and friends.
