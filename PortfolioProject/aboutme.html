About Me

Creative Developer | Student | Tech Hobbyist

Academic Information

After managing to attain my diploma at Westborough High School in 2022, I spent two years at UMass Boston as a computer science major. Upon entering 2024, I yearned for a change of scenery. I managed to seize the opportunity of transferring into UMass Amherst as a mathematics major before finally switching to informatics, the major I intend to graduate with.

Beyond the Classroom

When I'm not sitting behind a computer and coding until my fingers fall off, I enjoy reading, traveling, and spending time with my friends. I find that with all the time I spend looking at a screen, activities that allow me to break away from my computer for a while are invaluable. Though I am trying to break into new hobbies like art and video editing (despite admittedly arriving at the scene a bit late), I will always find comfort in a nice vacation, a captivating book, and quality time with my family and friends.
